Our Story

Benchmark Signs and Gifts is a family owned and operated company located in beautiful Chagrin Falls Ohio. We are committed to creating the finest custom carved signs for our customers. Your satisfaction is paramount to us. We specialize in personalized signs and gifts for any and all occasions. Our designer creates your custom sign using artistic knowledge , along with state of the art CAD software. Our shop uses everything from hand tools to CNC routers to create your custom sign. Each sign is then hand finished , fitted with hanging hardware and shipped to you.
